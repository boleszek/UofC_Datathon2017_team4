Exploraing relationship between poverty demographics and ridership¶

We wanted to explore the relationship between poverty and ridership. We calculated the difference in poverty index from year 2015 to year 2011 as well as the difference in ridership between those two years. Each point below represents these values for a particular zip code including a station and a demographic region.
